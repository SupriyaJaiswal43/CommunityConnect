Introduction to Hum Raahi Foundation (HRF)

Hum Raahi Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting, preventing, and fighting against sexual abuse in India, while providing comprehensive support to survivors. We aim to create a safe environment for everyone where they can establish themselves without fear. The organization is actively focused on the increasing cases of child sexual abuse in the country and its impact on the mental health of children at a tender age. We strive to create awareness and provide a safe space for those affected.

Hum Raahi is a guiding light for people going through the darkest phases of their lives. Reflecting the essence of our name, we are 'The Companion' and the port in a storm they seek. We are willing to travel down this road and ensure professional, financial, mental, and legal support to the victims of this heinous crime.

The prevalence of child sexual abuse in India and the lack of awareness among children became the driving force to start this organization. It is a safe space where victims can share their experiences, understand the brutality of the crime, and take appropriate action. Many cases in the country go unreported to protect the family's reputation, and sometimes families are in denial about the crime. The resistance of the victims and their families became a necessary impetus for us to work for this cause.

We provide a team of professionals, including lawyers to offer legal aid and psychologists to facilitate the recovery process for victims experiencing post-traumatic stress from sexual abuse, as well as medical professionals to address physical health needs and provide medical support. Hum Raahi Foundation implements a range of initiatives, such as conducting online and offline awareness campaigns on sexual abuse, prevention strategies, and more, along with support services like counseling, legal aid, and medical assistance for the victims.

You can also become a beacon of hope for those around you and help them fight the battles they are afraid to talk about. Join us in this journey of creating a better place for everyone. Even if it is hard, it will be worth it.
