Breaking the Silence: Taboo Talk with Swapna Foundation

The Hum Raahi Foundation kickstarted its outreach initiatives in collaboration with the Swapna Foundation through an impactful Taboo Talk event. This session focused on breaking the silence around the Kolkata case of Sexual Abuse. The event was well-received, with attendees actively engaging and sharing their thoughts, reaffirming the importance of open dialogue on such sensitive topics. This encouraging response boosted the team’s confidence and inspired a stronger commitment to reaching more children in need of support and education.

Community Voices: Daan Utsav at Hazratganj Metro Station, Lucknow

During Daan Utsav, as the Sewa Mela came to life, Hum Raahi Foundation (with 15 other organisations) hosted an impactful event at the bustling Hazratganj metro station in Lucknow. This initiative focused on gathering insights from the public on ways to improve child safety and advocacy. Passersby were invited to anonymously share their experiences of childhood harassment, including details about whether it was perpetrated by someone they knew. Volunteer registration forms were also made available, leading to an overwhelming response from individuals eager to support our cause. The event was a testament to the community's collective desire to create a safer environment for children and reinforced our commitment to tackling Sexual Abuse.

Empowering Young Minds: Good Touch and Bad Touch Session at Awadh Study Circle

In collaboration with Kanchan Kaya Mahila Sansthan, Hum Raahi Foundation conducted an enlightening session at Awadh Study Circle, focusing on the critical topics of good and bad touch, child safety, and personal boundaries. Through these methods, the foundation empowered participants with essential knowledge and confidence to protect themselves and recognize unsafe situations. This interactive approach fostered an atmosphere of trust and learning, leaving the children better equipped to navigate their surroundings safely.
