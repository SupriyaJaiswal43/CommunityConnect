OUR STATEMENTS

MISSION

The purpose of Hum Raahi is to prevent, protect, and fight against sexual abuse in the country and create a secure environment where people can thrive without fear. Our aim is to empower victims of sexual abuse to speak out without fear and to support them in dealing with the mental, emotional, and physical stress caused by their traumatic experiences.    Read More

VISION

Hum Raahi aspires to work not only for vulnerable sections of society but for all the people irrespective of gender and age. The aim is to empower as many people as possible and create nationwide awareness so that people in every corner of the country can understand the severe consequences of such heinous crime.   Read More

VALUES

At Hum Raahi Foundation, we are guided by our core values. Compassion drives us to approach each individual with empathy and respect. We maintain Integrity through honesty and transparency in all our actions. Our commitment to Empowerment helps individuals reclaim their lives with support and resources.   Read More
