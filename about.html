ABOUT US

Hum Raahi Foundation is a community where all families, regardless of their definition, can live free from violence.

"Empowering Lives, Eradicating Violence."

At Hum Raahi Foundation, we envision a world where every individual and family can live free from violence and abuse. Dedicated to fighting sexual abuse, we provide advocacy, education, and support to survivors and their loved ones. Our mission is to raise awareness, foster community engagement, and offer essential resources to create a safer, more inclusive society. Together, we are committed to breaking the silence and empowering those affected by abuse to reclaim their lives and thrive in a violence-free environment.
