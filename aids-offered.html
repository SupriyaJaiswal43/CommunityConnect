Aids Offered by Hum Raahi Foundation

To ensure the safety and well-being of every child affected by sexual abuse, we offer the following aids:

Representation

Legal aid organizations or attorneys specializing in child advocacy provide representation for victims in court proceedings, ensuring their voices are heard and their rights protected.

Medical Care

Ongoing medical care addresses physical injuries, sexually transmitted infections, or other health issues resulting from the abuse.

Mental Health Support

Comprehensive mental health support, including counseling and therapy, to help survivors cope with the emotional and psychological impact of abuse.

Training Professionals

Training for teachers, healthcare providers, law enforcement, and other professionals on recognizing signs of abuse and responding appropriately.
