Empowering Young Minds: Good Touch and Bad Touch Session at Awadh Study Circle

Event Overview

Date: 17th October

Location:Awadh Study Circle

In collaboration with Kanchan Kaya Mahila Sansthan, Hum Raahi Foundation conducted an enlightening session at Awadh Study Circle, focusing on the critical topics of good and bad touch, child safety, and personal boundaries. The event featured engaging role-playing activities, visual aids, and open discussions, creating a safe and supportive environment for the children. Through these methods, the foundation empowered participants with essential knowledge and confidence to protect themselves and recognize unsafe situations. This interactive approach fostered an atmosphere of trust and learning, leaving the children better equipped to navigate their surroundings safely.
