Our Story

Dear Friends,

I am honored to share with you the story behind the creation of Hum Raahi Foundation. This journey began with the painful and often silent experiences shared by those closest to meâ€”friends, family members, and acquaintances who revealed their stories of childhood sexual harassment. These were not just stories; they were cries for understanding, healing, and justice, buried deep within the hearts of those who had suffered in silence for too long.

Hearing these stories over and over again made me realize how widespread this issue is, yet how little it's spoken about. The pain and trauma these individuals carried made it clear to me that something needed to be done. I knew that if we continue to stay silent, the cycle of abuse would continue unchecked, leaving countless children vulnerable and alone.

Hum Raahi Foundation was born out of this realization. Our name, "Hum Raahi," which means "We are companions," reflects our commitment to walking alongside survivors on their journey toward healing. We are here to support, to listen, and to fight for those who have suffered. But our mission doesn't stop there. We also aim to raise awareness, educate communities, and advocate for stronger protections for children. Our goal is to break the silence, to create a society that acknowledges the pain of survivors and works tirelessly to prevent such abuse in the future.

One of the key aspects of our foundation is providing access to professional psychological support. We understand that healing from such trauma is a complex and deeply personal process. That's why we've partnered with experienced psychologists who specialize in trauma recovery, to ensure that survivors receive the care they need to rebuild their lives. Our aim is to create a safe space where they can find not just understanding, but also the tools to heal and move forward.

Through Hum Raahi Foundation, we aim to build a network of compassion, understanding, and action. Together, we can create a world where every child feels safe, heard, and valued. I invite you to join us on this journey, as we work together to make a difference.

With hope and determination

Founder

Deepak Singh
